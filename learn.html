Reading

Learning to read in English involves understanding descriptive text. Descriptive text aims to explain in detail about a person, place or thing. In reading lessons, you can read and understand descriptive texts to develop reading skills and expand their vocabulary.

Speaking

Speaking is one of the important skills in English that needs to be learned. To learn speaking, you need to practice speaking English regularly. You can start by practicing simple sentences in everyday conversation. Apart from that, you can also expand your vocabulary and develop grammar by reading and listening to English texts 

Listening

Listening is an important skill in learning English, to learn listening, you need to practice listening to English regularly, you can start by listening to audio or video in English. Apart from that, you can also expand your vocabulary and develop your grammar by reading and watching English texts.
