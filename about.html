English is the most widely used international language in the world. More than 400 million people around the world speak English as their first language. English is used in various fields, such as business, technology, science and entertainment. Therefore, it is important for someone to learn English in order to be able to communicate with people from various countries and cultures, as well as expand career opportunities. In addition, English also facilitates access to information and knowledge available on the internet. In Indonesia, English proficiency is still a challenge, even though Indonesia has great potential in improving the quality of human resources who master English. Therefore, it is important for everyone to learn English properly and correctly in order to broaden horizons, increase career opportunities, and communicate with people from various countries and cultures..
